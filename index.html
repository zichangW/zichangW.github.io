This file contains the tasks for assignments 6 and 7, including the flow model, hmw questions, 2 presona examples & 2 storyboards. See below for more details.

Flow Model for the Healthcare System:

User Registration and Profile Setup: Users sign up and create profiles, providing necessary medical information.

Appointment Booking: Users search for doctors, check their availability, and book appointments.

Virtual Waiting Room: Users enter a virtual waiting area before their appointment time.

Consultation: Users have a video consultation with the doctor at the scheduled time.

Prescriptions and Follow-ups: Doctors issue prescriptions or advice, and users can access follow-up information or appointments.

HMW Questions:

  1. How might we enhance communication and collaboration between users in the system?

  2. How might we streamline the user journey to ensure a seamless experience?

  3. How might we personalize interactions to cater to individual user preferences and needs?

  4. How might we mitigate potential conflicts or misunderstandings in user interactions?

  5. How might we leverage technology to improve accessibility for all users?

  6. How might we gather and implement user feedback effectively to iterate and enhance the system?

Project description:

Project Background:

This project is based on a previous project that collected interview transcripts and prospective user survey data from potential users of the Twitter site. The aim of this project is to understand the needs, preferences, behaviors and satisfaction of potential users of the Twitter website in order to provide a basis and recommendations for the improvement and promotion of the Twitter website.

Project Purpose:

The purpose of this project is to conduct data analysis on the interview records and potential user survey data of potential users of the Twitter website collected to extract valuable information and findings, as well as to segment and profile potential users of the Twitter website, so as to provide reference and guidance for the positioning and marketing strategy of the Twitter website.

Ø Of the notifications given, New followers, Likes, and Mentions are the most popular, accounting for 73.33%, 60%, and 53.33% of the total active users.

In general, most people have a relatively positive attitude towards Twitter’s performance of algorithm personalization.

\3) Write a paragraph to describe what we learned: themes, categories, things that

are unexpected or surprised us

Based on the survey data collected, several key themes and findings emerged:

\1. First, users vary widely in their engagement duration on Twitter, with most of them active for more than two years, suggesting a sense of loyalty among the user base. Interestingly, the distribution of user duration shows peaks at both ends, with 20% of users joining recently (within 6 months) and another 20% being long-term users (more than 5 years). This dichotomy may provide opportunities for targeted engagement strategies for both new and long-term users.

\2. The second topic is about the preferred device for accessing Twitter. It is worth noting that more than half of daily active users access the platform through mobile devices, which highlights the importance of optimizing the mobile app experience. Desktop computers followed at 33.33 percent and tablets at 6.67 percent. This perspective is crucial for application developers, emphasizing the need to focus on enhancing the mobile user experience to cater to the majority of users.

\3. The third topic concerns user engagement with notifications. New followers, likes, and mentions were the most popular notifications, accounting for 73.33%, 60%, and 53.33% of the total active users, respectively. This demonstrates the strong desire of the user community for exposure, approval, and attention. Conversely, the low percentages of options such as retweet, trend, recommended content, and direct messages indicate that users place relatively low importance on these types of notifications. This meticulous understanding of user preferences can guide feature development and content prioritization to align with user expectations and improve overall satisfaction.

Valuable feedback that could have a significant impact on product strategy was obtained through this survey. As we move forward, these findings will guide our efforts to increase user engagement, improve the mobile app experience, and prioritize features that match user expectations, ultimately ensuring a more satisfying and tailored Twitter experience for all users.

3)Write a paragraph describing what you learned: themes, categories, things that

are unexpected or surprised you.

From the interview records of Twitter users mentioned above, we can learn many interesting information about Twitter usage and preferences. The main themes include Twitter’s usage motivation, users’ views on the platform, their interactions and experiences on Twitter, and their suggestions for future improvements to the platform.

Topic 1: Twitter usage and preferences

Motivation for using Twitter: The main motivations for users to use Twitter include accessing real-time news, managing social media, understanding and sharing world affairs, and expressing personal opinions and ideas.

Platform attractiveness: Users believe that Twitter is attractive because of its concise and fast features, as well as real-time and global coverage. This enables users to quickly connect to interested groups and topics.

Usage balance: Users emphasize the balance between reading content, interacting with others, and posting their own tweets when using Twitter. There are also some differences in the focus and duration of different users.

Topic 2: Twitter usage experience and feedback

Favorite aspect: Users like Twitter’s real-time and global coverage, as well as its ability to quickly connect with different groups of people. They also emphasized the importance of establishing valuable interactions and connections on the platform.

Continuous use motivation: The motivation for continuous use includes obtaining fresh information, promoting business, showcasing personal value, and interacting with interesting people. Users believe that this continuous experience is a source of satisfaction and achievement for them.

Interactive Impact: Users described meaningful communication with experts and other users, as well as the positive impact of these interactions on expanding knowledge and perspectives.

Theme 3: Twitter integration and enhancement

The importance of integration: Users emphasized the importance of seamless integration between Twitter and other social media platforms to improve management efficiency. This includes the need for one-stop management, cross platform publishing, and content sharing.

Improvement suggestions: Users have put forward some suggestions for improving the Twitter experience, including improving content curation, simplifying conversations, adding sound and video features, and adding translation or subtitle functions.

Unexpected or surprising events:

Different usage motivations: Although all users are on Twitter, each user’s usage motivation is different. Some are for professional needs, some are for news, some are for socializing, and some are for learning. This diversity demonstrates Twitter’s characteristics as a multifunctional platform.

Concerns about privacy and security: Users’ concerns about data privacy and security are a common theme. This indicates that users are very concerned about personal information security on social media platforms, especially for business users, which is an important component of business reputation.

Suggestions for future improvements: Among the improvement suggestions proposed by users, they involve Twitter’s voice and video functions, content translation, and improved content curation. These suggestions demonstrate users’ expectations for more diverse and enriched platform features, which may have an impact on Twitter’s future development.

Through these interviews, we have learned that users have diverse needs and expectations for Twitter usage motivation and experience. Users value real-time, global connectivity, effective social media management tools, and platform security and privacy protection. In addition, users have also put forward some constructive suggestions for future improvements on Twitter, which may help the platform better meet the diverse needs of users. Overall, these interviews provide insights into the Twitter user community and provide useful references for platform improvement and innovation.

Project Proposal

project background

Twitter, as a social media platform, has a broad user base, but our team believes that there are still potential user groups that have not been fully explored. By collecting interview records and survey data from potential users, we can better understand their needs, interests, and concerns, in order to develop more targeted market strategies and improve user growth rates.

Project purpose

This project aims to understand the needs, preferences, behaviors, and satisfaction of potential users of the Twitter website, in order to provide basis and suggestions for the improvement and promotion of the Twitter website.

Project Method

This project will use the following two methods for data collection:

Interview method: Conduct semi-structured interviews with potential users via phone or video to understand their understanding, attitude, usage, and expectations of the Twitter website. The interview will be conducted according to a pre designed interview guide, which includes the following main questions:

Topic 1: Twitter Usage and Preferences

What motivated you to use Twitter, and how frequently do you engage with the platform?

Q2:Why do you find Twitter appealing, and for what purposes do you primarily use it?

Q3:How do you balance your time between reading content, engaging with others, and posting your own tweets on Twitter?

Q4:What features or aspects of Twitter keep you engaged, and why are they important to you?

Q5:How do you manage any challenges or drawbacks you’ve encountered while using Twitter?

Topic 2: Twitter Experience and Feedback

Q1:What do you like most about your experience with Twitter, and why?

Q2:Why do you continue to use Twitter, and what keeps you coming back?

Q3:How would you describe your interactions with other users on Twitter, and what impact have these interactions had on you?

Q4:What challenges or aspects of Twitter, if any, do you find unsatisfactory, and why?

Q5:How do you envision Twitter fitting into your broader online presence and social media strategy?

Topic 3: Twitter Integration and Enhancement

Q1: How important is it for Twitter to seamlessly integrate with Twitter and other social media platforms in your daily routine?

Q2:What features or changes on Twitter could enhance your experience or make it more useful for your needs, and why?

Q3:How would you like Twitter to help you manage your online presence on Twitter and other platforms?

Q4:What data privacy and security concerns, if any, do you have when using social media, and why are they important to you?

Q5:What specific ways do you see Twitter adding value to your use of Twitter and other social media platforms, and why?

Survey method: Collect quantitative data from potential users through online questionnaires, such as their basic information (age, gender, occupation, etc.), their satisfaction with the Twitter website, their frequency and duration of use of the Twitter website, etc. The questionnaire will be conducted based on a pre designed questionnaire template, which includes the following main parts:

How long have you been using Twitter? [Less than 6 months, 6 months to 1 year, 1-2 years, 2-5 years, More than 5 years]
How frequently do you use Twitter (daily, weekly, monthly, rarely)?
What is your primary device for using twitter？(Mobile, Desktop, Tablet)
How often do you engage with tweets by liking or retweeting them? [Frequently, Occasionally, Rarely, Never]
How satisfied are you with the overall design and user interface of Twitter? (Scale: 1-5, 1 being very unsatisfied, 5 being very satisfied)
Do you prefer a minimalist or feature-rich interface in a productivity app? (1 =
Minimalist, 5 = Feature-Rich)
What type of notifications are most important to you on Twitter? [Select all that apply: New followers, Mentions, Likes, Retweets, DMs, Trends, Recommended content, News]
How relevant do you find the content in your Twitter feed? (Scale: 1-5, 1 being not relevant, 5 being highly relevant)
How well do you feel Twitter’s algorithms personalize content for you? (Scale: 1-5, 1 being poor, 5 being excellent)
Are you satisfied with the privacy settings and options on Twitter? [Yes, No, Neutral]

Project Process

This project will proceed according to the following process:

Step 1: Design an interview guide and questionnaire template. Design appropriate interview guidelines and questionnaire templates based on project objectives and methods to ensure effective collection of the required data.

Step 2: Determine the sample. Determine appropriate sample sizes and sampling methods based on project objectives and scope to ensure representativeness and reliability of data. For example, methods such as stratified random sampling or convenient sampling can be used.

Step 3: Implement data collection. Arrange interview time and methods based on the sample situation, and send questionnaire links to potential users. During the data collection process, pay attention to recording the interview content and questionnaire collection, and promptly address any problems or difficulties encountered.

Step 4: Write a report. Based on the results of data analysis, write a detailed project report, including project background, objectives, methods, processes, results, conclusions, and suggestions, and attach relevant data tables and charts.

This project is expected to achieve the following goals:

Understand the needs, preferences, behaviors, and satisfaction of potential users of the Twitter website, in order to provide basis and suggestions for the improvement and promotion of the Twitter website.

Improve the visibility and attractiveness of the Twitter website, increase its potential and active users, and enhance its market share and competitiveness.

Establish good relationships with potential users, enhance their trust and loyalty to the Twitter website, and promote their word-of-mouth dissemination and recommendation of the Twitter website.
